PUQE (Pregnancy-Unique Quantification of Emesis) is a validated scoring system for nausea and vomiting during pregnancy. Track your symptoms daily to monitor changes and share with your healthcare provider.

Troubleshooting

App Won't Open

  • Force close the app and reopen it
  • Restart your device
  • Check for iOS updates
  • Reinstall the app (Note: This will delete all local data)

Notifications Not Working

  • Check iPhone Settings ‚Üí Notifications ‚Üí Corgina
  • Ensure "Allow Notifications" is enabled
  • Check Do Not Disturb settings
  • Review notification schedules in the app

Photos/Voice Not Working

  • Check permissions in iPhone Settings ‚Üí Corgina
  • Enable Camera, Microphone, and Photos access
  • Restart the app after granting permissions
